warning_button

(./warning_button.m)

Function Synopsis

warning_button(message)

Help text

 WARNING_BUTTON puts a warning button on the screen
   Call by warning_button(message)
 Note that the button is very firmly on the screen due to the
 ('WindowStyle','modal') property. In fact, apart using a callback to reset
 the 'WindowStyle property, I know of no way to get rid of the warning
 message short of restarting the window manager.
